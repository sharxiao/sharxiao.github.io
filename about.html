ABOUT ME

Hi there, I'm Sharon!

I'm a UX designer with 2+ years of experience who's passionate about building digital products with a human-centred approach. During this time, I've worked in the FinTech, real estate, education, and non-profit industries.

Currently, I'm designing at helloart, an art-tech company that is reimagining the art discovery and buying experience. I'm also a soon-to-be graduate and a 4th-year Management student at the University of Toronto, with a specialization in Information Technology and a minor in Applied Statistics.

In my spare time, you can find me working my way through my long list of books to read, exploring the world of Zelda, or trying out new restaurants.



What does design mean to me?

I believe that design is a bridge that connects people together. It serves to find a deep understanding of people that can't be found in any other place. In my personal experience, I have been very fortunate to have met and collaborated with others in different crafts through design.

I also believe that good design is intuitive. It shouldn't be unnecessarily complex nor should it require the user to rack their brains trying to figure the design out. When I design, I make sure to incorporate user research and feedback into my work to validate my ideas.

I'm passionate about simplifying the tough things in life with thoughtful design, making smarter decisions quicker, and bringing diversified voices to the table.



Okay, that's enough about me! With all there is to learn, I'm always open to exploring new opportunities. If you're interested in connecting, please feel free to reach out on LinkedIn or through email. My resume is also available upon request.
