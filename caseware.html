I spent 8 months working at Caseware, a FinTech company that helps businesses in the accounting and auditing industry work more efficiently and derive insights with its software solutions. From January to August of 2021, I worked on the Smart Engagements team at Caseware as a UX Designer Co-op.

In this role, I focused on projects that revolve around automating accounting and audit processes to better help our users complete their tasks faster and more efficiently. I also had the chance to work on design projects for our internal design team that explored our design process and identified our values as a group.

While the projects I worked on during my co-op are under an NDA, feel free to reach out to me and I'd be happy to talk about my experience at Caseware!
