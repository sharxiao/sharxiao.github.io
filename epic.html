During the summer of 2022, I spent the summer working at Epic Investment Services. From May to August, I worked on the Marketing & Communications team at Epic as a Design Co-op.

In this role, I designed various graphics, branded merch, wayfinding signage, and web pages. I had the opportunity to work on multimedia projects with a dedicated team and gained valuable experience in corporate design + branding.



Renfrew Centre website


One of the highlights of my co-op at Epic was redesigning the website for the Renfrew Centre, one of Epic's retail properties. I had the chance to lead the redesign from conception to creating the final prototype. During the length of this project, I worked closely with the property manager, VP of Marketing and Communications, and developer.
