In the last quarter of 2022, I started working at African Impact Initiative as a UI/UX Designer. Specifically, I worked with the team on one of its initiatives, African Impact Challenge (AIC).

AIC is an annual challenge based in Africa that invites early-stage entrepreneurs to build and pitch their venture with an experienced team. Teams receive funding, mentorship, and other resources to grow their business.

As my work at African Impact Initiative is under a confidentiality agreement, I'm unable to share all the details, but please feel free to reach out if you're interested in learning more about my experience!





What I worked on


During these few months, I had the opportunity to work on various tasks to build the foundation of Venture Build in light of launching the MVP (minimum viable product). In particular, I focused on user research and drawing insights from our findings to aid in product decisions.
